Box Contents and Ordering Information
-------------------------------------

**VAB-820**:

* 1 x VAB-820 embedded board (with Freescale i.MX ARM Cortex-A9)
*  1 x DC-in power cable
*  1 x Audio cable
*  1 x USB cable
*  1 x COM cable (3-wired for debugging the console)
